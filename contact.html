Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monster Survivors really free to play?

Yes! Monster Survivors is 100% free to play. No downloads, no registration, and no hidden fees. Just visit our website and start playing!

Can I play on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our game is optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Play anytime, anywhere on any modern web browser.

How do I report a bug?

Please send bug reports to support@monster-survivor.online with details about what happened and any relevant information to help us reproduce the issue.

Do you accept feature requests?

Yes! We love hearing from our community. Send your suggestions to contact@monster-survivor.online. While we can't implement every idea, we carefully consider all feedback.

Can I use your game content?

All game content is protected by copyright. Please contact us at business@monster-survivor.online for any licensing or partnership inquiries.

How often is the game updated?

We regularly release updates with new features, balance changes, and improvements based on player feedback. Check our blog for the latest news!
