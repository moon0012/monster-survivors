FAQ & Common Questions: Everything You Need to Know

Welcome to our comprehensive FAQ page! We've compiled answers to the most frequently asked questions about Monster Survivors. Whether you're troubleshooting issues, looking for gameplay tips, or just curious about game mechanics, you'll find helpful information below.

Gameplay Questions

Gameplay
How do I control my character in Monster Survivors?

Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to move your character around the battlefield. Your mouse cursor determines which direction your character faces. The best part? Your weapons automatically target and fire at nearby enemies, so you can focus entirely on movement and positioning!

Gameplay
How do I level up and get stronger?

Defeat monsters to collect experience gems that drop throughout the battlefield. As you collect these gems, your experience bar fills. When it reaches full, you level up! At each level-up, you'll be presented with upgrade choices that significantly enhance your character's abilities.

Gameplay
What's the best strategy for surviving longer?

The golden rule of Monster Survivors is never stop moving. Constant movement keeps you from being surrounded by enemies. Additionally, prioritize area damage weapons early in the game, collect every experience gem you can safely reach, and learn to read enemy attack patterns to dodge effectively.

Technical Questions

Technical
Is Monster Survivors free to play?

Yes! Monster Survivors is completely free to play in your web browser. No download, no registration, and no payment required. Simply visit the website and start playing instantly!

Technical
What devices and browsers can I play Monster Survivors on?

Monster Survivors runs on any modern web browser including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. It works on desktop computers (Windows, Mac, Linux) and mobile devices (iOS, Android). For the best experience, ensure your browser and operating system are up to date.

Technical
The game is lagging or running slowly. What can I do?

If you're experiencing performance issues, try these solutions:

  • Close other browser tabs and applications that might be using system resources
  • Lower your browser's graphics quality settings if available
  • Ensure your browser and graphics drivers are updated
  • Try a different browser—some perform better than others depending on your system
  • Check your internet connection, as Monster Survivors requires an active connection

Progression & Features

Progression
Does my progress save between sessions?

Monster Survivors is a roguelike game, which means each run starts fresh. However, the game may track your high scores and achievements. The roguelike nature ensures that every playthrough offers a unique experience and encourages experimentation with different builds and strategies.

Features
Are there different characters to play?

Yes! Monster Survivors features multiple unique characters, each with distinct starting stats, abilities, and preferred weapon types. Characters include The Warrior (balanced fighter), The Ranger (precision striker), and The Speedster (swift survivor), among others. Experiment with different characters to find your favorite playstyle!

Features
How many weapons are available in the game?

Monster Survivors features 15+ unique weapons that can be upgraded through multiple tiers. These include projectile weapons like Blaster, area damage weapons like Explosion Sphere, melee options, status effect weapons, and utility options. Each weapon offers distinct playstyles and synergies to discover!

Boss & Difficulty

Difficulty
When do bosses appear in the game?

Boss encounters occur at regular intervals, typically every 5 waves. Bosses include The Swarm Lord (Wave 5), The Flame Golem (Wave 10), The Shadow Assassin (Wave 15), and more challenging bosses at higher waves. Each boss has unique attack patterns and requires specific strategies to defeat.

Difficulty
Does the game get harder over time?

Yes! Monster Survivors features progressive difficulty. Each wave brings stronger and more numerous enemies. Monster types evolve, new enemy varieties appear, and the challenge increases continuously. Your upgrades and improved skills are essential for keeping up with the escalating difficulty!

Mobile & Controls

Mobile
Can I play Monster Survivors on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Monster Survivors is fully playable on mobile devices. The game features touch controls optimized for smartphones and tablets. Simply use the virtual joystick on your screen to move your character. The game adapts seamlessly to both desktop and mobile platforms.

Controls
Can I customize the controls?

Currently, Monster Survivors uses fixed control schemes optimized for gameplay balance. On desktop, you can use either WASD or arrow keys for movement. On mobile, the virtual joystick provides intuitive touch controls. We're always listening to feedback, so suggestions for control customization are welcome!
