Cookie Policy

How we use cookies to enhance your experience

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Think of cookies as the website's memory: They remember your preferences, login details, and how you use the site, allowing us to provide you with a better, more personalized experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes to enhance your experience on Monster Survivors Online:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Maintain user sessions, ensure security, and provide basic functionality Session / 1 year
Performance Collect analytics data to understand how visitors use our site 2 years
Advertising Deliver personalized ads and measure ad campaign effectiveness Variable
Functionality Remember language preferences and other user settings 1 year

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Game session management
  • Access to secure areas of the website

Note: These cookies cannot be disabled as they are essential for the website to operate.

3.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by providing information about:

  • Pages visited most often
  • Error messages encountered
  • Time spent on each page
  • Navigation patterns through the site

This information helps us improve the performance and design of our website.

3.3 Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to:

  • Deliver ads based on your interests
  • Measure ad campaign performance
  • Prevent fraudulent clicks
  • Limit the number of times you see the same ad

4. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may use third-party cookies provided by our partners, including:

  • Google AdSense: For advertising purposes
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance tracking

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies. We encourage you to review these policies to understand how they use your information.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies.

5.1 How to Disable Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from particular websites
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser
  • Block all cookies from being set

5.2 Browser-Specific Instructions

To manage cookies in your browser, visit the relevant help pages:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Important: If you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly, and you may not be able to access certain content or services.

6. Google AdSense and Cookies

As a participant in the Google AdSense program, we use Google's advertising cookies. Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the Internet.

You can opt out of interest-based advertising by:

  • Visiting Google's Ads Settings
  • Using the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out page
  • Using the European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out page
